# Lab 02 · Design the Pet Library Schema

> 📁 [`projects/pet-library-schema`](../projects/pet-library-schema)

Design the full schema for the Pet Library in SDL — types, enums, an interface, unions, inputs, payloads, queries, and mutations.

All instructions and requirements are in the project README: [`projects/pet-library-schema/README.md`](../projects/pet-library-schema/README.md).

Work schema-first: no resolvers, no server. When you're done, compare your design decisions with [`complete/schema.graphql`](../projects/pet-library-schema/complete/schema.graphql) — differences aren't wrong, but be able to defend your nullability choices.

**Debrief questions** (we'll discuss as a group):

- Where did you use `!`, and where did you deliberately not?
- Interface vs. union — where did each earn its place?
- What does your `logIn` mutation return, and why?
